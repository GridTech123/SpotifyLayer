Here is a list of known errors and how to fix them

Song name/artist not updating

cause: Something on spotify's side.
fix: Pause and play the song.

Song name/artist says an error occured

cause: You started spotify layer while an ad was playing, the song name has an unsupported character.
fix: There isn't one, you just need to wait for the ad to finish or for the next song to play.

Spotify layer sits on low FPS mode

cause: Your computer doesnt have the resources to run Spotify Layer.
fix: Close other running apps (especially games). If that doesnt fix it your computer is likely not powerful enough to run Spotify Layer.

lyrics not loading

cause: Musix match dosen't have that song in their library. Somtimes this is caused by songs having featured artists because of musix match's way of embedding songs.
fix: Look up the song on musix match
